Stanford History Blog

Leland Stanford Purchases Land

1876
Leland Stanford purchases 650 acres of land, which will become the Stanford University campus. Future purchases will add to the property, leading to over 8000 acres total.

University Established

1891
Stanford University is established. The first class has 559 men and women.

Earthquake Devastates Campus

1906
The great San Francisco Earthquake of 1906 devestates the campus, permanently changing Stanford's architectural character.
